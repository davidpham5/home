About Me

Hello there. I'm David Pham. I'm a frontend developer for a tech company in Washington DC. I've been a constantly learning since 1984. And I love coffee brewed from an Aeropress.

My design sensibilities are heavily influenced by several people. I often revisit the work of Dieter Rams, and his ten principles for good design. His principles are something I continue to study over and over whenever I run into a tough challenge.

I really admire Jason Santa Maria and his hard work around typography. He really inspired me in making the web more beautiful.

Jen Simmons is someone who has done some exceptional work on advancing CSS to a new era. Her work in web standards, CSS Grid, and An Event Apart presentations impacted me in becoming a better frontend developer.

I've put together my resume as AngularJS 1.5 app. It illustrates something as simple as a resume can be done with great type and engineering. When you're done looking around, lets get some coffee and talk shop.
