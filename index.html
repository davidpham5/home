About Me

Hello there. I'm David Pham. I'm a frontend designer, headphones collector, and aero-pressed coffee enthusiast currently residing in Washington DC.

My design sensibilities are heavily influenced by several designers. The work of Dieter Rams, and his ten principles for good design continue to guide me in making things more useful for people.

Much of my taste comes from transforming traditional print to web design. Jason Santa Maria showed ideas from print such as, proper typography, and brought them to the web. He really inspired me in making the web more beautiful through type.

Jeffrey Zeldman is a personal hero of mine. His work in web standards showed the power and beauty of CSS, the clarity in semantic HTML, and the insistence for writing cleaner code.

I've kept a small portfolio to show off some things I did in the past. When you're done looking around, lets get some coffee and talk shop.
