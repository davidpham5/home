About Me

Hi. I'm David Pham. I'm a frontend designer, champion of the user, and areo-pressed coffee enthusiast currently residing in Washigton DC. I help people create exceptional interfaces through human centered design.

I have a preference for Anonymous Pro as my fixed width type for my code editor, Sublime Text 3 (former Coda 2 user). Promixma Nova is my favorite font. #333 is a nice contrast against #fff for readability. Simplicity requires discipline and restraint. I try to avoid being too clever. These are just some of my values. I hope we can argue together and build something great.

I've kept a small portfolio to show off some things I did in the past. I usually cringed at how inexperience I was. I made a lot mistakes, failed, and sighed at my ignorance. I've learned a lot and I am thankful for those who helped me along the way. When you're done looking around, lets get some coffee and talk shop.
